About Count Masters

Count Masters is an exhilarating stickman running game that combines strategy, timing, and quick reflexes. As you navigate through challenging levels, you'll collect colorful stickmen to build your crowd, multiply your numbers through strategic gates, and face off against obstacles that will test your decision-making skills.

The game features intuitive controls that make it easy to pick up but challenging to master. Each level presents unique obstacles, from spinning hammers to deadly spikes, requiring you to think fast and choose the right path to preserve your crowd and reach the finish line with maximum numbers.

With its vibrant graphics, smooth animations, and progressively challenging gameplay, Count Masters offers endless entertainment for players of all ages. Whether you're looking for a quick gaming session or hours of addictive fun, this game delivers an engaging experience that keeps you coming back for more.

How to Play Count Masters

Basic Controls: Use your mouse or touch controls to guide your stickman crowd through the course. Move left and right to navigate around obstacles and collect additional stickmen.

Collect & Multiply: Gather blue stickmen scattered throughout the level to grow your crowd. Pass through multiplication gates to significantly increase your numbers - choose wisely as some gates multiply while others subtract!

Avoid Obstacles: Watch out for spinning hammers, spikes, and other deadly traps that can reduce your crowd size. Time your movements carefully to minimize losses.

Reach the Finish: Navigate through all obstacles and reach the finish line with as many stickmen as possible. The more you have, the higher your score and the better your chances of advancing to the next level.

Pro Tips: Look ahead to plan your route, prioritize survival over collection when facing difficult obstacles, and remember that sometimes taking a longer but safer path pays off in the end!
